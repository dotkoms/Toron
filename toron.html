Roadmap

Phase 1 – Testing

Using SNS we currently have access to we plan on making a fluid and easy method for uploading sites to Solana network. SNS is not tailored for this kind of transaction nor has this been thoroughly tested to be the most beneficial method of data storage/viewing. We are going to be going through R&D for basic sites with minimal functions. our main goal in the testing is to make sure we give everyone a straight path for data upload for anyone that would like to host their own site.

 

Phase 2 – Building of our Domain name system

Our first stage to our goal for decentralized internet to be accessible for everyone is setting up a DNS. a DNS is like .com, .net etc. This allows us to setup our domains to be easily searchable and appeal to Web2 and Web3 web surfers alike. Once our DNS is up and running we are making our domains purchasable. users can Make websites and upload their sites using their assets.

 

Phase 3 – Site builder/smart contract templates

On top of setting up domains to be owned and used by our users we want to be a one stop shop for decentralized internet. our next step after our DNS is a Site builder with a variety of website templates and smart contracts for various use cases like marketplaces, mint and reoccurring subscriptions.

 

Phase 4 – expansion

Using a DAO structure we plan on making a branch of our protocol that is run by the community! this means all decisions of this branch will be decided by DAO vote and all revenue will be kept in the DAO and disbursed on how they see fit. This gives way for a community lead project in the Toron family which we back with resources and expertise.
